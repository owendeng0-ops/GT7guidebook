# GT7 Track Atlas Bilibili Video Brief

## Video Goal

制作一条约 75 秒的 B 站功能介绍视频。重点不是版本迭代过程，而是让 GT7 玩家快速看懂这个网站现在能做什么，并邀请他们反馈数据、交互和训练流程。

## Suggested Title

我做了一个 GT7 赛道布局训练助手：查赛道、定圈速、记练习、选车辆

## Suggested Description

GT7 Track Atlas 是一个离线可打开的赛道布局级训练助手，目前支持：

- 41 个 GT7 赛道地点、121 个官方布局检索
- 中文赛道名对照、区域 / 难度 / 训练状态筛选
- 布局级赛道图切换，长度、弯角、直道和校验信息同步变化
- 每个布局独立记录目标难度、个人最佳圈速、状态、收藏和笔记
- 圈速指南和车辆推荐，说明推荐原因
- 弯角 hover 热点标注，正在从纽伯格林开始校准

想听 GT7 玩家建议：

- 哪些赛道应该优先补弯角点位？
- PS5 个人圈速和截图数据，怎样同步到网页最顺手？
- 车辆推荐应该按 PP、轮胎、驱动形式还是赛事类型来组织？
- 这个工具更应该偏资料库，还是偏训练计划？

## Hyperframes Prompt

Create a 75-second 16:9 Chinese Bilibili product walkthrough for a web app named "GT7 Track Atlas". The video should be feature-focused, fast-paced, and practical. Do not frame it as a version history or development diary.

Show the current product workflow:

1. Open with "GT7 Track Atlas" and describe it as a layout-level GT7 training assistant.
2. Show the feature map: track search, layout maps, training records, car recommendations.
3. Show search and filters: 41 tracks, 121 official layouts, region filters, difficulty filters, training status filters, Chinese track names.
4. Show Nürburgring layout switching: GP, Nordschleife, Endurance II; emphasize that map and stats change per layout.
5. Show the training card: target difficulty, best lap, status, favorite, notes, local storage.
6. Show pace guide and car recommendation cards with reasons.
7. Show corner hover hotspots on the Nürburgring map.
8. End with viewer feedback questions.

Visual style: dark racing telemetry UI, cold blue panels, red and cyan accents, crisp data cards, subtle scan lines, fast but readable transitions. Keep text short and functional.
